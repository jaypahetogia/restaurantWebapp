About Us

Established in 1992, we have been Wellington's dessert and delicacy icon

Our restaurant, founded by renowned pastry chef Sonic Johnson, offers a unique blend of classic and contemporary dessert creations. Each dish is crafted with locally sourced, premium ingredients, ensuring a fresh and sumptuous experience for every customer. At Sonic's Desserts, we believe in creating an ambiance that's as delightful as our desserts, offering a cozy, inviting space where friends and family can gather to savor our delectable treats. Join us for an unforgettable dessert journey, where each bite is a celebration of flavor and artistry.
